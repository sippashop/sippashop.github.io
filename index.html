Policies & Terms

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your Sippa tea experience! If for any reason you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we're happy to help. You can return unopened tea packages within 7 days of receiving your order for a full refund. Please note that we cannot accept returns on opened tea packages for health and safety reasons, and we don't offer exchanges. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to an error on our part. Simply contact us at hello@sippa.shop to start your return process - we'll make it as easy as possible!

Shipping Policy

We currently ship within the United States only. Most orders ship within 2 business days unless otherwise specified on the product page. Shipping times may vary depending on your location and the specific product ordered. We'll send you tracking information once your order ships so you can follow your tea on its journey to you!

Refund Policy

Refunds for eligible returns will be processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your returned items. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for your purchase. For any questions about refunds or if you need assistance with a return, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at hello@sippa.shop - we're here to help!

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. We collect only the information necessary to process your orders and provide customer service. We do not sell, share, or rent your personal information to third parties except as necessary to fulfill your orders (such as sharing shipping information with our shipping partners). All payment processing is handled securely through our payment processor.

Quality Commitment

We stand behind the quality of our tea products. All tea is carefully prepared and packaged to ensure freshness at the time of shipment. For optimal freshness and flavor, store your tea in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. If you have any concerns about the quality of your tea, please contact us immediately at hello@sippa.shop.
